# Triangle Ventures

The best things come in threes.

Triangle Ventures is a thesis-driven fund built around one organizing principle. Every investment is pressure-tested against a three-part framework: three conditions that define the problem, three structural advantages in the solution, three paths to market dominance. Not a superstition. A discipline that forces founders to think with clarity most investors never demand.

## The framework

Three sectors per fund. Three companies per sector per vintage. Three-year active support window per investment. The constraint applies at every layer so the discipline compounds across every partner, every check, and every portfolio company.

## Why it works

Most venture funds differentiate on network or check size. Almost none differentiate on intellectual identity. A fund with a genuine organizing thesis creates a recognizable lens that compounds into a portfolio that looks genuinely different from everything around it.
